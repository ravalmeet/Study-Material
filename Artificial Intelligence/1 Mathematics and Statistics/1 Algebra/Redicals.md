## Basic Rules
- **Product Rule:** \(\sqrt{ab} = \sqrt{a} \cdot \sqrt{b}\)
- **Quotient Rule:** \(\frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}} = \sqrt{\frac{a}{b}}\)
- **Power Rule:** \(\sqrt[n]{a^m} = a^{\frac{m}{n}}\)
